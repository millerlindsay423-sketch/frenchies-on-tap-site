Meet the Frenchies

Two Very Different Personalities

One's in charge. One has no idea what's going on. Both are extremely food-motivated.

Palmer

The Boss

Dramatic older sister energy. Runs every brewery visit like she owns the place, because as far as she's concerned, she does. Certified connoisseur of treats and self-proclaimed beer enthusiast, she's toured over 100 breweries across the Southeast — rating each one on water bowl quality, treat offerings, and how friendly the staff are. Because what's great beer without a welcoming atmosphere?

"I don't do 'quick stops.' I do arrivals."

Teamwork makes the dream work. Mostly.

Rosie

The Chaos Goblin

Knocks over one (1) thing per brewery, minimum. Rookie card is still in production. Give her a minute, she's new.

"What's that? Is that mine now?"

The Mission

Every Brewery Stop Has a Bigger Purpose.

Every brewery stop is a chance to raise money for Shepherd's Men and the SHARE Military Initiative. It's a free program that helps post-9/11 veterans living with PTSD and traumatic brain injury rebuild their lives.

$35,000

puts one veteran through the full SHARE program. Palmer's goal is to help get there, one brewery at a time.
