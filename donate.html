Why SHARE?

The SHARE Military Initiative helps post-9/11 veterans transition to civilian life by addressing PTSD and traumatic brain injury (TBI) through mental health support, resilience training, and holistic recovery methods. It's a free program — funded entirely by donations like the ones Palmer raises one brewery stop at a time.

"Every water bowl, every treat, every brewery card — it all adds up to something bigger than beer."

What Your Donation Does

Tailored Treatment — Donations go directly to personalized care plans that include therapy and resilience training designed to meet the unique needs of veterans facing PTSD and TBI.

Community Connection — Support creates a network where veterans can connect with and support each other, fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie.

A Brighter Future — Contributions help veterans build sustainable lives, so they can chase their dreams and make positive contributions to their communities.

Give once, or set up a recurring donation — either way, you're helping get one more veteran through the door.
