About Palmer

Hi, I'm Palmer — a French Bulldog, a certified connoisseur of treats, a self-proclaimed beer enthusiast, and a canine ambassador for good behavior (and good beer). I tour breweries across the Southeast with my humans, and in a good year I've hit almost 100 of them.

I rate every stop on the things that actually matter: water bowl cleanliness and freshness, treat quality, staff friendliness, and the overall vibe. Bonus points for belly rubs and ear scratches. Because if the atmosphere is off, it's like serving a five-star meal on a paper plate — doesn't matter how good the beer is.

"I don't have a tail to wag, but I make up for it in opinions. Some of the beer I've sampled tasted like it was brewed in a sock. Most of it was great. I have a system."

These days I'm not touring solo — my sister Rosie comes along for the chaos. But every brewery stop, every rating, every treat negotiated out of Lindsay, is in service of something bigger than the next great IPA.

The Mission

Hey there, fellow adventurers! It's me, Palmer, the Patriotic Beer-Loving Frenchie — and I'm on a mission that's close to my furry heart: raising money for Shepherd's Men to support the SHARE Military Initiative.

$35,000

sponsors one veteran through the full SHARE program. That's the standing goal — no year attached, just the next veteran we can help.

SHARE helps post-9/11 veterans transition to civilian life by addressing PTSD and traumatic brain injury (TBI) through mental health support, resilience training, and holistic recovery methods. Every dollar raised goes toward ensuring another veteran gets the top-notch care they need.

What Your Donation Does

Tailored Treatment — Donations go directly to personalized care plans that include therapy and resilience training designed to meet the unique needs of veterans facing PTSD and TBI.

Community Connection — Support creates a network where veterans can connect with and support each other, fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie.

A Brighter Future — Contributions help veterans build sustainable lives, so they can chase their dreams and make positive contributions to their communities.

Whether it's a one-time gift or a recurring donation, every bit helps get one more veteran through the door.
