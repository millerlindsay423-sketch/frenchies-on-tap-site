Asheville, NC

Burial Beer Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"It was crazier than a dog park on a Saturday!"

Alright, fur-friends, gather 'round because I've got a tale about this brewery that had me wagging my tail and scratching my head at the same time! I really wanted to give this place a higher rating because my humans were raving about the beers, but it was crazier than a dog park on a Saturday! I mean, it felt like the entire town of Asheville decided to throw a party at once.

Now, let me tell you, the vibe here is super cool! They've got these vintage car frames you can hang out in, all decked out with stickers like they're the coolest cars in town. And the outdoor deck area? Massive! It's like a dog's dream playground, but on December 31, it was 70 degrees!

But here's the kicker: they only had a walk-up bar open for beers, which meant the line was longer than my list of favorite treats! My humans had to make quick decisions, so they got five different IPAs to try, but we let the beertender pick for us. So, guess what? We have no idea what we actually tried!

Now, let's talk about the canine scene—this place was a doggy paradise! I spotted FOUR English bulldogs, which is like finding a unicorn in the wild! And the kids running around were all about giving me pets! I even handed out a bunch of my stat cards like I was a celebrity!

So, while I think we need to do some additional research on this place, it's definitely a fun spot for pups and humans alike! Just be prepared for a bit of chaos and a lot of love!
