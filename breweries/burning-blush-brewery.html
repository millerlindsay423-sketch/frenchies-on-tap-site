Burning Blush Brewery

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

""I met you last year!" — Melody, the brewer."

🐾🎉 This was our second time visiting Burning Blush, and let me tell you, it was just as pawsome as last year! As soon as we walked in, Melody—the brewer and Whit's mom—exclaimed, "I met you last year!" I mean, what's better than being recognized a whole year later? I must be a celebrity in the dog world! 🌟🐶

Melody was a total sweetheart, showering me with pets and maybe sneaking me a few extra treats. 😍🍖

Burning Blush was a favorite last year, and it didn't disappoint this time either! First off, their place is super cool—like, vintage bar cool! The Lannings found this gem of a literal vintage bar and had it shipped all the way from Chicago! Plus, they've got stunning stained glass windows and a beautiful outdoor area where I could soak up the sun while my humans enjoyed their brews. ☀️🍻

Now, let's get to the good stuff—BEER! Whit seems to be all about American IPAs and lagers, and my humans were all about the Square Your Luck and Hostage to My Feelings this year. 😂🍺

But seriously, not only are they brewing some fantastic beer, but the folks at Burning Blush are just the nicest people! So, if you find yourself in the area, do yourself a favor and stop by. Cheers to good brews and even better company! 🐾🍻✨
