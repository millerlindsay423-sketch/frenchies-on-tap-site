Salt Face Mule Brewing Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"This place is HUGE! It's got everything you could ever want: a brewery, a restaurant, an outdoor area, and even mini golf!"

🐾🎉 Hey there, my fellow snack enthusiasts! It's your favorite furry foodie here to tell you about one of our Asheville adventures in munchie land! After an intense online hunt, we decided on the Salt Faced Mule! 🐴🍻

Let me tell you, this place is HUGE! It's got everything you could ever want: a brewery, a restaurant, an outdoor area, and even mini golf! It's definitely a family-friendly spot, which is great because when a kid drops a snack – I'm a fan! And guess what? They even have COCKTAILS! 🍹💃

Now, here's the kicker: this venue isn't exactly dog-friendly, so I had to don my service dog harness and go to work. Ugh, you know how I feel about that! 😒🐕

The humans kicked things off with some fried cauliflower, a burger, and chicken tenders — there was enough to feed a small army! 🍔🐔

Now, let's get to the important stuff: the drinks! They tried the Sit a Spell Pale Ale and the Holler Atcha West Coast IPA, both of which were worth another taste. And don't even get me started on the Strawberry Jalapeño Margarita! 🌶️🍓

So, if you're ever in the area and looking for a place to munch and sip, the Salt Faced Mule is definitely worth a visit! 🐾👀🍽️
