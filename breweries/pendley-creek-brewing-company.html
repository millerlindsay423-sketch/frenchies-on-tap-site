Pendley Creek Brewing Company

🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4.5 / 5)

"Pendley is all about the purist beers, and they do it right!"

So, let me tell you about our find in Jasper, GA! We met Daniel and Pam while property hunting in North Georgia, and now we make it a point to visit Pendley Brewery every few weeks. It's like our little slice of heaven in downtown Jasper, and trust me, it's worth the trip!

Now, if you're looking for jalapeño-infused, fruity concoctions that taste like a smoothie gone wrong, you can keep on searching. Pendley is all about the purist beers, and they do it right! You won't find any weird flavors here—just some seriously awesome traditional brews that make my humans' taste buds do a happy dance. My humans have basically tried the whole menu, and on a scale of 1 to 5, they're all sitting pretty above 3.7, with a few actual 5's thrown in for good measure. So, you can bet your bottom biscuit that you won't be disappointed!

The vibe at Pendley is small and quaint, like a cozy little den where you can kick back and relax. And if you're visiting in the fall, you can bet your favorite chew toy that there's a good football game on the TV. Now, we may have some friendly debates about Daniel's hometown team, but I'll let that slide because he's known to share a chip or two with yours truly. I mean, who can resist a Frenchie?

So, if you're in the area and looking for a place that serves up fantastic beers, a cozy atmosphere, and the occasional snack for your favorite four-legged friend, Pendley Brewery is the place to be! Just remember to give Daniel a little nudge for those chips—he's got a soft spot for me, and I'm not above using my charm to score some tasty treats! Cheers to good brews and even better company! 🍻🐾
