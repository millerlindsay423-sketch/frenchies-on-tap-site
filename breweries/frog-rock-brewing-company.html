Frog Rock Brewing Company

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"They brew "Happy Beer" with lithium-rich mineral water."

At the recommendation of my buddy Justin at Skint Chestnut, we hopped over to Austell, GA, to meet Justice and his dog, Miss Moo Moo. Now, I know she has a fancy name that's probably longer than my leash, but let's be real—Miss Moo Moo is way more fun to say!

Talk about dog-friendly! We rolled in, and this girl was ready to play. We were instant besties—#BFFs for life! Sundays are our special day because that's when my friend is guaranteed to be there, and I can't resist a good game of chase. Frog Rock may not be huge, but they have a super cool outdoor space in the back that's perfect for sniffing all the smells and leaving little messages for my fellow canine visitors. It's like my own personal doggy playground!

Justice is just as cool as Justin, and he knows the people and their tastes better than I know where my humans hide the treats. The humans rave about their beers, but I'm just here for the popcorn—my ultimate weakness! They don't have dog treats per se, but if you find a tiny human, you're bound to score some of that buttery goodness. Just make sure to give them your best puppy eyes; it's a foolproof strategy!

The vibe at Frog Rock is also super chill. Not a single visit has gone by without my humans and I making new friends at the bar. Sometimes, we even take them along on our next adventure—because who doesn't want to hang out with a French bulldog on a mission?

Now, here's the kicker: the unique vision of Head Brewer and Co-founder Ryan. He's brewing what he calls "Happy Beer." Why? Because their water comes from Lithia Springs Mineral Water, which contains trace amounts of Lithium. Yes, you heard that right! So, as you sip on a Frog Rock beverage, you might find your spirits soaring higher than a squirrel in a tree! Alcohol is usually a natural depressant, but thanks to the Lithium in your beer, you might just feel like you can conquer the world—or at least the nearest dog park!

So, if you're into a cool vibe, good beer, and a unique vision that might just make you feel like a million bucks (or at least like you've had a few too many belly rubs), go check out Frog Rock Brewery! Cheers to good brews, great friends, and the best popcorn around! 🍻🐾
