All Sevens Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"If you're looking for good eats, tasty brews, and a full bar, then All Sevens is the place to be!"

🐾🎉 Hey there, my fellow adventure seekers! It's your favorite furry foodie back at it again to tell you about another gem we discovered in Asheville! If you're looking for good eats, tasty brews, and a full bar, then All Sevens is the place to be! 🍻🍽️

Now, I have to be honest—this spot isn't totally dog-friendly, but since it was a gorgeous 65-degree December day, my humans and I decided to soak up the sunshine on the patio. And let me tell you, I took my role as the official greeter very seriously! I was racking up pets like it was my job—TONS of them! I should probably start charging for my services! 😎🐕

Our server and beertender, Amanda, was an absolute gem! She was all about the pets and had some great conversation going. I mean, who doesn't love a human who knows how to scratch the right spots? 🙌🐾

Now, my humans decided to go all out with the ultimate beer taste test—tasters of every beer on tap! Good thing I'm not drinking, because someone had to keep an eye on their shenanigans! And guess what? Their favorites actually complemented each other! One human was all about the ESB, giving it a solid 4 stars, while the other was head over heels for the American IPA. They also thought the Kolsch and Hefeweizen were worth a second taste. 🍺✨

But wait, it gets better! The humans tried the Gringo Tacos, and let me tell you, they were blown away by how tasty those little bundles of joy were. 🌮🤤

Oh, and did I mention the HUGE sticker wall? You know I had to put the Frenchies up there! We also spotted two of our favorites, Horned Owl and Buckbald, hanging out on the wall. It's always super cool to see some familiar faces out in the wild! 🦉🎉

So, if you're ever in Asheville and looking for a place to kick back, enjoy some sunshine, and indulge in tasty food and drinks, All Sevens is a must-visit! 🐾❤️
