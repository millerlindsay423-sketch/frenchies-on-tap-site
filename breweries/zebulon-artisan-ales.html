Zebulon Artisan Ales

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"I'm basically a celebrity now!"

🐾🎉 Hey, party pals! It's your favorite four-legged adventurer to spill the beans about our third stop of the morning: Zebulon! Nestled in the adorable little area of Asheville, right next to Leveller and Eluvium, this place was destined to be a blast! 🎈🍻

We decided to bring along our new buddies, Kasey and Rhett, because let's be real—beer tasting is always better with friends! As soon as we walked in, I knew we hit the jackpot! There were four canine pals already hanging out, and the beertender wasted no time in giving me some serious love. She even took my sticker and baseball card and added me to the beer fridge! I'm basically a celebrity now! 🐶✨

Now, this was the last stop before nap time, so the humans were starting to wind down. But they still managed to taste the Imperial Red Ale, and they thought it was pretty darn good! 🍺😄

So, if you're ever in Asheville, make sure to swing by Zebulon! With furry friends, friendly beertenders, and tasty brews, it's a paw-some place to hang out. 🐾❤️
