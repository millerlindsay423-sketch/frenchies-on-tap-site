Copperhill Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"I was basically the star of the show!"

🐾🎉 Oh la la, fur-friends! Let me tell you about my latest adventure at Copperhill—it was a delightful surprise! We stopped by because my humans had some time to kill before visiting our pals at Buckbald. And let me tell you, this place was busier than a squirrel convention! It was a bit tricky to read the blackboards and get the beertenders' attention, but once my humans snagged some seats at the bar, it was all butt wags and good vibes! 🍻✨

Now, this place is HUGE! I'm talking about leather sofas that are so comfy, I could nap there for days. They were decked out for the holidays, and I swear I saw some stockings hanging at my eye level. I was convinced there were treats in there just waiting for a Frenchie like me to snag them! 🎄🐶

And guess what? There were tons of Frenchie lovers at Copperhill! I met a super nice Frenchie family who showered me with pets, and another couple who couldn't get enough of my adorable face. I was basically the star of the show! 🌟❤️

But let's get to the real reason you're here: the beer! My humans tried the Hazy Consequences and Valhalla. Now, let's talk about Valhalla… I mean, if you know me, you know I'm all about that military and patriotic life! It's like they brewed this beer just for me! Both IPAs were solid picks, and I can confirm they'd definitely go back for round two. 🍺

Just a little heads up: check the train schedule before you go because this place gets packed! And trust me, when the crowd rolls in, getting service can be harder than getting a treat out of my humans! So, if you're in the area, swing by Copperhill for some good brews and even better vibes! 🐾❤️✨
