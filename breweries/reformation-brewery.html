Reformation Brewery

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"Why not strut our stuff to downtown Woodstock and hang out with our pals at Reformation Brewery?"

🐾🎉 Hey there, here to spill the beans about our latest adventure in the sunny ATL! So, Atlanta's February weather decided to throw a party with a balmy 65 degrees, and my humans and I thought, "Why not strut our stuff to downtown Woodstock and hang out with our pals at Reformation Brewery?" 🌞🍻

Let me tell you, this place has the location game on LOCKDOWN! It's right off the Woodstock trails, smack dab in the heart of downtown, and guess what? They've got an open container law! That means my humans can sip and stroll while I sniff and schmooze. Plus, there's a gigantic outdoor lawn area—perfect for families and pups like me to frolic and make new friends. I even got to meet some fellow Frenchies! It was like a mini Frenchie convention, and I was the guest of honor! 🐶🎈

But before we dive into the beer, I've got to give a shoutout to my fur-tastic friends, Spencer and Paula Nix, the dynamic duo behind Reformation. These two are five stars all the way! Not only have they created an amazing community vibe, but they're also like superheroes for our military and first responders. They're always the first to raise their paws for Shepherd's Men and have raised a ton of dough for the SHARE Military Initiative. Every Memorial Day, they brew a special beer just for Shepherd's Men and throw a massive party in Woodstock. Talk about a butt waggin' good time! 🎉🐾

Now, let's get to the good stuff—BEER! One of the coolest things about Reformation is that they distribute, so you can snag your favorite brews at the grocery store. They've even added some non-alcoholic options and craft cocktails for those humans who aren't as into the hoppy goodness as I am. A couple of brews that are high on our list are A Cold One, Alani (a wine-inspired sour that's as unique as my snout), and the Jude. 🐕💃

And let's not forget the new kid on the block—the Dawn IPA! We were especially excited about the Pride and Prejudice Imperial Stout made in collaboration with Alma Coffee. I mean, who doesn't love a good book reference with their brew? 📚🍺

So, if you're looking for a paw-some place to soak up some rays, meet fellow Frenchies, and enjoy some top-notch brews, Reformation Brewery is where it's at! Trust me, you won't want to miss it! 🐾❤️ Cheers to good times and even better friends! 🍻✨
