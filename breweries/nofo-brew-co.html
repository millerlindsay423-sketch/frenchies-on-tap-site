NoFo Brew Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"Knowing life isn't fair, I don't think it's fair that kids can play fetch and I can't BUT I'm always welcome – and they let me on the couch!"

Hey there, fellow fur-friends and human companions! It's your favorite brew-hopping Frenchie, Palmer, back with another tail-wagging review! Today, I'm excited to share my thoughts on a pawsitively fantastic spot: NoFo Brew Co! If you're looking for a place that's got it all—great drinks, a cozy atmosphere, and plenty of space for us pups—then this is the place for you!

What I love most about NoFo Brew Co are the comfy sofas that make for the perfect lounging spot after a long day of sniffing and exploring. And let's not forget the gigantic play area! It's like my own personal dog park, where I can run around and show off my best zoomies. Plus, there's a fire pit that's just perfect for those chilly evenings when my humans want to cozy up with a drink while I bask in the warmth. It's a win-win!

Now, let's talk about what my humans enjoy. They're big fans of the craft cocktails here, and I can see why! One of their favorites is the "Big Chili," made with Agave Blanco, fresh citrus, and a pineapple Tajin and salt rim. They also can't get enough of the "Lone Wolf," which combines vodka, jalapeño, peach, pineapple, and lime.

Of course, NoFo Brew Co is also known for its always-available IPA, "Snow Ghost." My humans rave about it, and I can tell it's a crowd-pleaser! But what really makes this place special is the consistent presence of a food truck. That means there are always tasty nibbles to find on the floor—score! And let's be honest, where there are little humans (and there are plenty at NoFo), there are bound to be snacks dropping all around. It's like a treasure hunt for me!

The family vibe at NoFo is another reason we love it so much. It's a place where everyone feels welcome, and I get to meet all sorts of new friends—both human and canine! Whether it's a beautiful day to lounge outside in the large play area or a chilly evening by the bonfire, NoFo Brew Co hits the spot every time.

Oh, and did I mention they have two other locations with equally cool vibes? But we frequent the Cumming spot since it's practically a hop, skip, and a jump away for us.

In conclusion, if you're looking for a paw-some place to enjoy great craft cocktails, delicious food, and a welcoming atmosphere, NoFo Brew Co is the place to be! So grab your humans, head on over, and let's raise a glass (or a bowl) to good times and great brews! Cheers!
