NoFo Brew Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"Knowing life isn't fair, I don't think it's fair that kids can play fetch and I can't BUT I'm always welcome – and they let me on the couch!"

NoFo Brew Co's Cumming location is a regular stop for Palmer — dog-friendly seating throughout, a large play area, and a fire pit that makes it worth the drive even in cooler months. Try the "Big Chili" or "Lone Wolf" cocktails, or the "Snow Ghost" IPA, a staple on the taplist. Food trucks rotate through regularly and the whole vibe is family-friendly. Palmer's one gripe: kids get to play fetch and she doesn't. But she's allowed on the couch, so it evens out.
