Schoolhouse Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4.5 / 5)

"If only this place were a little closer to home, it would definitely be a top contender for my favorite brewery!"

Oh la la, fur-friends! If only this place were a little closer to home, it would definitely be a top contender for my favorite brewery! I mean, it could still make the cut, who knows? First off, this place is HUGE! It's like a wonderland for pups and humans alike, with all kinds of awesome spots to explore! 🌟🐶

The vibe here is super cool, and the decorating… Let's just say it's got more character than a squirrel in a tutu! They've got pinball machines, table tennis, and even beer pong for those humans who want to entertain their kids or just relive their college days. 🎱🍻 And don't even get me started on the seating! Old school bus seats, tons of tables and chairs, and an outdoor space that's just begging for a Frenchie sprint! While I'm not supposed to be off-leash, my humans have been known to let me do a few laps when no one's around. I mean, a Frenchie's gotta stretch those legs, right? 🏃‍♂️💨

And speaking of kids, they're like well-behaved little pups themselves! Just kidding! But honestly, for such a big place with so many entertainment options, it's like a schoolhouse for humans and their little ones. I approve! 📚🐾

Now, let's get to the good stuff—BEER! 🍺 I've got to give a shoutout to my favorite beertender, Easa! She really knows how to make a Frenchie feel special. Always ready with a solid recommendation, she's like my personal beer concierge! The great thing about Schoolhouse is they always have at least 15-20 beers on tap, so there's something for everyone! For my humans who are all about those IPAs, they've got plenty of options to keep them happy. Right now, the most exciting brews on the menu are the Full Tilt Boogie, Big Dad Energy, and some Aleuminati. I mean, how can you resist a name like that? 😂✨

And for those non-beer drinkers, they've got some tasty seltzers on tap too! So, whether you're a beer lover or just along for the ride, Schoolhouse has got you covered. Until next time, I'll be dreaming of this place and plotting my next visit! 🐾🍻❤️
