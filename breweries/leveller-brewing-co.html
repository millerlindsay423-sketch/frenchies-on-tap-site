Leveller Brewing Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"The beer was good, the patrons were great, and the company was even better!"

Hey there, fur-friends! Palmer here, and let me tell you about another Asheville New Year's 2024 adventure at Leveller, a spot recommended by a fellow human at Eluvium. Apparently, this little corner of Asheville is like a brewery treasure map, with three places all within walking distance! 🍻🐶

Now, Leveller is a cozy little joint—definitely Frenchie-friendly, but sadly, no treats for us pups. But the best part? We met the cutest couple ever, Kasey and Rhett! They're just like my humans—no kids, but they love to hop from brewery to brewery like it's a game of fetch! I racked up the pets from them! 🐾❤️

Rhett was all about the stouts, while Kasey was vibing with my humans and enjoying the IPAs. The humans tried the Jake on the Puppy and the Four Horned, and both got some serious tail wags from our crew! But the Four Horned really stole the show. 🍻🍑

So, to sum it all up: the beer was good, the patrons were great, and the company was even better! Definitely worth a visit if you're in the area. 🐾✨
