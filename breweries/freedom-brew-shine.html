Freedom Brew & Shine

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"If you're on the hunt for a destination brewery that's more fun than a squirrel on a caffeine high, look no further than Freedom Mill!"

Listen up, humans! If you're on the hunt for a destination brewery that's more fun than a squirrel on a caffeine high, look no further than Freedom Mill! This place is so family-friendly that even the kiddos will be entertained while you sip on your favorite libation. I mean, they've got enough activities to keep the little ones busy while you enjoy your drink—it's like a daycare for humans! Just remember to keep an eye on them; you don't want them trying to "help" with the ax throwing!

One reason my humans are head over paws for Freedom Mill (besides my almost-famous status, of course) is that they serve both beer and cocktails. So, if you're one of those rare creatures who doesn't like beer (I know, shocking!), you've got plenty of options to mix it up. It's like a buffet for your taste buds!

And let's talk about dog-friendliness! They've got a dog bowl with a Frenchie on it—because obviously, they know who the real star is here. Plus, there's always a food truck parked outside, which means special treats are just a sniff away. I've been known to do a little dance when I see certain trucks roll in.

This place is HUGE! They've got everything from a store to a shooting range, an arcade upstairs, and even ax throwing! I mean, who doesn't want to throw sharp objects while sipping on a cold brew (JUST KIDDING, one thing before the other, you know?) I like to think of myself as the unofficial mascot, lounging on the big sofa in front of the jumbo TV, watching the big game while my humans cheer like they're at the Super Bowl.

Shoutout to JB, the general manager, and Maria, the social media guru! These two are the best! They always have treats and pets for me, which is basically the doggie equivalent of winning the lottery.

And let's not forget the patriotism! This place has it locked down tighter than my grip on a chew toy. As the most patriotic Frenchie you'll ever meet, I'm all in for the red, white, and blue! So, if you're looking for a place where you can enjoy great drinks, entertain the kiddos, and let your pup feel like a VIP, Freedom Mill Brewery is the place to be! Cheers to good times, great brews, and endless belly rubs!
