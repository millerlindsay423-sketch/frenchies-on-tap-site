Crooked Culture Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4.5 / 5)

"This should be more but lots of unleashed children!"

Woof woof, fellow fur-friends and human companions! It's your favorite brew-hopping Frenchie, Palmer, here to share my thoughts on a pawsitively delightful spot: Crooked Culture Brewery in Cumming, GA! Now, let's get one thing straight—when it comes to brewery ratings, it's all about location, location, location! And Crooked Culture is nestled in the primo City Center, making it a tail-wagging destination for both humans and their four-legged pals.

First off, let's talk about the humans. While they rave about the fantastic beers (and trust me, we'll get to that), I can't help but notice all the shopping options nearby! My humans love to stroll through Top Knotch Boutique, Pieces and Peaches, and Rugged and Refined. The best part? The lovely ladies and gents at these shops are always ready to give me a belly rub and a tasty treat! It's like a treasure hunt for my humans, and I get to enjoy the spoils!

Now, onto the main event—Crooked Culture Brewery! As soon as you step inside, you're greeted by a cozy "living room" vibe, complete with large leather sofas and a big TV to catch our favorite games. It's the perfect spot for my humans to kick back and relax while I soak up all the attention. And let's not forget about the food trucks that often park outside, serving up delicious bites to complement those frosty brews!

Speaking of brews, my humans have a soft spot for IPAs (like, duh, who doesn't?), and Crooked Culture has some real winners on tap. Two that really stand out are "Sounds and Gestures" and "This One Slaps." They're always raving about how hoppy and refreshing they are! But here's the kicker—my humans are also fans of the Santa's Slay Ride Imperial Stout. They say it's like Christmas in a mug, and I can't argue with that! If only they'd share a sip with me (just kidding, I'll stick to my treats)!

In conclusion, if you're looking for a paw-some brewery experience with great beer, a comfy atmosphere, and plenty of shopping and dining options within walking distance, Crooked Culture Brewery is the place to be! So grab your humans, head on over, and let's raise a glass (or a bowl) to good times and great brews! Cheers! 🐾🐾
