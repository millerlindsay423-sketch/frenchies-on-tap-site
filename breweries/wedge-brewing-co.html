Wedge Brewing Co

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"The location is pretty awesome! Lots of cool shops nearby for the humans to browse."

🐾🍻 Here's the scoop on our quick pit stop at Wedge Brewing! This was a drive-by sip-and-sniff mission because it was right on the river and super convenient to the other spots we were hitting. And let me tell you—the location is pretty awesome! Lots of cool shops nearby for the humans to browse, plus a great outdoor area perfect for some good ol' fashioned people-watching.

The weather? Absolute perfection. ☀️ So, of course, we snagged a spot outside and soaked up the sunshine while watching some humans get way too serious about cornhole.

One thing that makes this spot extra cool? They've got guest taps and wine, so even if you're not all about the beer, there's still something tasty to sip on! 🍷🍺 We tried the Foundy Sesh—a solid, easy-drinking brew that gets a paws-up from us!

Would we come back? For sure! Next time, we'll stick around longer, sniff out some more beers, and maybe even challenge someone to a cornhole match—Frenchie style. 🐾🍻
