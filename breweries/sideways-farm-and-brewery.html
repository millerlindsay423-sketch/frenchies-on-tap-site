Sideways Farm and Brewery

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"Where the heck did my humans bring me? Is this a brewery or a dog park for goats?"

🐾🎉 Hey there, fur-friends! It's your favorite Frenchie, Palmer, here to spill the beans about our New Year's trip for 2024! We kicked things off at a little brewery in the middle of nowhere called Sideways Farm and Brewery. I won't lie, when we hopped out of the car, I was like, 'Where the heck did my humans bring me? Is this a brewery or a dog park for goats?' 🐐🚗

As soon as we stepped inside, I was hit with the aroma of mulled wine that made my taste buds do a happy dance! 💃🍷 And there was this big, poofy dog who totally ignored my attempts to make friends. Rude! 🙄🐕

Now, let's talk brews! These humans of mine should've asked Dawnelle, the brewtender, for recommendations right off the bat since this was our first stop of six! They brought home a Farm Stock Ale with caramel and toffee that was out of this world! 🤤

They also enjoyed the Sake Farmhouse Ale and an IPA on tap. I'm not sure what all that means, but if it makes my humans happy, I'm all for it! 🎉🍻

So, if you find yourself wandering in the area, make sure to check out Sideways Farm and Brewery! It's a hidden gem! Cheers to new adventures and tasty brews! 🐾🍻✨
