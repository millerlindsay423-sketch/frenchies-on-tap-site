Mountain Layers Brewing Company

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"It's got a cozy little bar area on the first floor and a rooftop patio that's perfect for soaking up the sun."

Alright, fur-friends, let me tell you about this super cool spot in downtown Bryson City! It's got a cozy little bar area on the first floor and a rooftop patio that's perfect for soaking up the sun. But here's the kicker: the owners opened this place after more than 30 years of public service…

They've got this massive Honor and Remember Wall filled with all kinds of awesome patches from military veterans and first responders. I mean, we all know I'm a big supporter of our vets, so this really warmed my little canine heart!

My humans tried the Diatribe Brewing collaboration of Cerebral Espionage and the Nydee Effret IPA. They said it was a great spot to stop by and catch a view.

Now, here's the catch: it's not totally dog-friendly—only service dogs allowed. So, I had to bust out my official gear and go to work. I'd much rather be strutting my stuff in my cute harness than actually doing a job! But hey, a pup's gotta do what a pup's gotta do!

So, if you're in the area and looking for a cool spot with a heartwarming vibe and tasty brews, this is the place! Just remember to bring your service dog gear if you want to join the fun!
