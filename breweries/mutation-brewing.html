Mutation Brewing

🐾🐾🐾 (3 / 5)

"This place is HUGE! I walked in and felt like a celebrity."

🐾🎉 Hey there, my fabulous fur-friends! I am back at it again with another butt shakin' adventure! This time, we ventured across 285 to Roswell Rd to check out Mutation Brewing. And let me tell you, this place is HUGE! I walked in and felt like a celebrity—everyone loves a Frenchie, am I right? There were tons of fellow Frenchie lovers and owners at their little market, and I was basically the main attraction! Move over, beer; it's all about the Frenchie fame! 🌟🐶

The beer tenders were totally on my wavelength, too. They had a cup of treats ready to go, which is basically the best kind of service. I mean, who needs a fancy cocktail when you can have a treat in every paw? 🍖🍻

Now, this was just a quick stop on our brewery tour, but my humans managed to squeeze in some tastings. They tried the Liquid Hop Magma, Sharks with Laser Beams, and a favorite, Hooked on Phoenix (Book 5). The Hooked on Phoenix was a super crisp, citrusy IPA that had my humans dreaming of the beach. I mean, I'd love to hit the beach too, but I'm more of a "chase the seagulls" kind of girl! 🏖️🐦

Mutation Brewing has a lot going on—there's an event space, a cool outdoor area with fun swing chairs, trivia nights, and the best part? ALL the dogs are invited! It's like a doggy paradise! 🐾🎈

While we didn't get to taste any of the food, they do have a kitchen, so you can get your eat on too. I mean, who wouldn't want to munch on some tasty bites while sipping on delicious brews? So if you're looking for a paw-some place to hang out with your humans and fellow pups, Mutation Brewing is the spot! Trust me, you won't want to miss it! Cheers to good times and even better treats! 🍻🐾✨
