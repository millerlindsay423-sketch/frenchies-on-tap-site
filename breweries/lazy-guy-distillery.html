Lazy Guy Distillery

Palmer's Pick 2025

🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4.5 / 5)

"Great drinks, a cool vibe, and a pup-friendly atmosphere? What more could a dog ask for?"

Tucked into a downtown Kennesaw building from the 1800s, Lazy Guy Distillery has river stones out on the patio for cooling off — a small detail that goes a long way when you're wearing a fur coat in Georgia summer. Server Tracy had the water bowl out before Palmer's humans even sat down, which is basically the whole ballgame as far as Palmer's concerned. Craft cocktails worth sticking around for too (the pumpkin spice mule and habanero honey mule get called out by name), and limited tours announced via social media for the humans who want the full experience. Great drinks, a cool vibe, and a pup-friendly atmosphere? What more could a dog ask for?
