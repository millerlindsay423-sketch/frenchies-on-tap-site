Riverside Rhapsody Beer Company

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"A party in my mouth. (Re: the BeBop Tango IPA.)"

🐾🎉 Hey, fur-friends! Let me tell you about a Sunday evening stop during our 2024 NYE Asheville trip. After a little power nap, we set out to explore some breweries that bravely weathered Hurricane Helene like champs! 🌪️🍻

Our first stop was Riverside Rhapsody. It was dark when we arrived, but I could already feel the good vibes! We definitely need to come back when the sun is shining because this place has a massive outdoor area that's just begging for some zoomies! Plus, rumor has it they have a resident BBQ company! 🍖🐶

As soon as we walked in, I knew I was in the right spot because there were two dachshunds dancing on a table like they were auditioning for "Dancing with the Stars"! Their human hooked me up with a treat that made my mouth water like never before: a HOTDOG! 🌭😍

We didn't realize it at first, but our beertender, Mike, is one of the owners! He gave us the scoop on how scary the hurricane was for the river area. We especially loved the BeBop Tango IPA—it was like a party in my mouth! 🎶🍺

So, halfway through this New Year's trip, I'm feeling like we need to plan another Asheville outing when the weather is warm! 🐾✨
