One World Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"Like a hip hangout where you could sip and chill for hours."

🐾🎉 Alright, fur-friends, let me tell you about this place that made me feel like I was sneaking into a secret speakeasy! I mean, you'd think it would be hard to find since it's basically in a basement, but nope! Just a little butt wiggle and a nose for adventure! 🕵️‍♂️🐶

Once inside, I could totally see how you could lose track of time. Speaking of treats, our beertender Mike was like a magician with snacks! He was Johnny-on-the-spot, ready to serve up some solid goodies. Major props to Mike for knowing how to win over a Frenchie! 🍖✨

Now, let's talk about the beer! My humans dove into a bunch of their IPAs and really loved the Citra Bomb IPA, Ashevegas, and the Aztec-nology IPA. The vibe here was super cool—like a hip hangout where you could sip and chill for hours. I can't wait to check out their other location next time we're in Asheville! 🥳🍻

So, if you're looking for a place that feels like a hidden gem and has treats for both humans and pups, this is the spot! 🐾❤️✨
