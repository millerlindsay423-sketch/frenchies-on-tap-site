New Belgium Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4.5 / 5)

"New Belgium totally blew me away!"

🐾🎉 Alright, fur-friends, let me spill the kibble about my latest adventure at New Belgium! Now, I'll admit it—I'm a bit of a big brewery snob. I usually prefer those cozy, intimate spots. But let me tell you, New Belgium totally blew me away! 💥🍻

First off, let's talk about their massive outdoor turf area. It's like a doggy playground, but with LOTS of free-range kiddos running around! Thank goodness there's almost a magical forcefield keeping them from entering the "Liquid Center." The back deck overlooks the river, and the view? Absolutely pawsitively beautiful! 🌊🌞

Now, I must have made quite the impression on my first visit—we went back for seconds. On our second visit, Belle, the manager, came right up to me and knew my name! We had an awesome chat with Belle, who filled us in on how New Belgium handled the hurricane situation. They really stepped up for their employees while they were closed for almost two months fixing things up. ❤️🐾

Now, let's get to the most important part—THE BEER! They do some truly awesome IPAs! The Juice Force, Tropical Force, and Juicy Haze were all tail-wagging favorites. This was the only brewery where my humans genuinely liked every single beer they tasted! 🥳🍺

So, if you're looking for a place that's got great vibes, stunning views, and some seriously delicious brews, you've got to check out New Belgium! 🐾❤️✨
