Ironmonger Brewing & Distilling

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"I'm not sure I get it – I can bring my treats and water, the beer is good, but I can't tell if they want us there."

Howdy, fellow fur-friends and human companions! It's your favorite brew-hopping Frenchie, Palmer, here to share my thoughts on a truly unique spot: Iron Monger Brewing! Now, let me tell you, this place is a bit of an adventure, but once you get past some of the oddities, you'll discover they craft some seriously great beers.

First off, Iron Monger is tucked away in an offbeat strip mall, so don't be surprised if you have to do a little treasure hunting to find it. And trust me, make sure to check their website for the hours—those can be one of the quirkiest things about this brewery! You might even stumble upon a pocket knife convention while you're there. Yep, you heard that right! It's all part of the charm of this place.

Now, if you're looking for some entertainment, Iron Monger has got you covered with ax throwing! Just a little tip from me: save your beers for later if you plan on trying your hand at that. I'd hate to see my humans miss out on their delicious brews because they got a little too carried away with the axes!

The vibe here is strong cowboy, with some fantastic murals from the famous 6666 Ranch in Texas adorning the walls. It really adds to the atmosphere and makes you feel like you're in a true Western saloon—just with better beer!

Speaking of beer, my humans have a couple of favorites that they can't stop raving about. The "Billet Blood Orange Ale" is a must-try, especially on those warm Southern afternoons. It's super refreshing and perfect for sipping while lounging in the sun. They also love the "You Have Feelings?" IPA, which packs a flavorful punch that keeps them coming back for more.

In conclusion, if you're up for a little adventure and don't mind some quirky surprises, Iron Monger Brewing is definitely worth a stop! With great beers, unique entertainment, and a fun cowboy vibe, it's a place where both humans and pups can have a blast. So grab your humans, head on over, and let's raise a glass (or a bowl) to good times and great brews! Cheers!
