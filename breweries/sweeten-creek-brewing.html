Sweeten Creek Brewing

🐾🐾🐾 (3 / 5)

"I was allowed in, but it felt like I was at a party where no one wanted to play with me!"

Alright, fur-friends, let me tell you about this brewery that had treats, beer, and BBQ! Now, I wasn't really feeling the treats—maybe because I was a little overserved at Burning Blush earlier in the day. My humans let me taste test their BBQ too and reality is… most times if the choice is BBQ or treats… I mean what would you pick? 🍖

Now, here's the kicker: I was allowed in, but it felt like I was at a party where no one wanted to play with me! I was practically giving them my best puppy eyes, and all I got was a few half-hearted pats. Rude! 😒🐶

The humans decided to do an IPA flight, and tried the Phresh Pulp, Tap Shack Session, AsheChill, and Bro, Chill. Their feelings about these brews were all over the place — it was like watching a tennis match, but with beer! 🎾🍺

So, was it worth the visit? Sure! The beer was pretty good overall, but they definitely missed the mark on some of my other doggy criteria—like, I don't know, a petting station or a treat buffet just for me? A Frenchie can dream, right? 🐾✨
