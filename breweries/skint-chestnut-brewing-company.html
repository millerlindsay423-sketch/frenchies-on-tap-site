Skint Chestnut Brewing Company

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (4 / 5)

"It's like a big, frothy family reunion where everyone's wearing funny shirts and talking about hops instead of politics!"

One of the things my humans and I love most about craft beer is the culture surrounding it. It's like a big, frothy family reunion where everyone's wearing funny shirts and talking about hops instead of politics! We get to chat with the brewers, the owners, and the brewtenders to hear their wild stories, what makes their brewery special, and where they go to drink when they're not busy brewing their own magic.

So, one sunny Sunday, we decided to explore an alternative side of town and stumbled upon Skint Chestnut. Let me tell you, it did not disappoint! It's nestled in the charming little downtown area of Powder Springs, GA, where the only thing more delightful than the beer is the friendly atmosphere.

When we arrived for our first visit (and all the subsequent ones, because we're basically regulars now), we were greeted by Justin. I'm pretty sure he sleeps on a cot in the back of the brewery because he's always there, like a dedicated beer guardian! Seriously, if there were a "Most Likely to Be Found at the Brewery" award, he'd win it paws down. Plus, he has an eidetic memory!

Justin remembers me, my humans, AND what they've tried, haven't tried, and what they like to drink. It's like he's got a little doggy database in his brain! Our top two picks are the Hazy IPA (five paws up for sure) and the Sabrovosspa (a Skint Chestnut exclusive that's so good it should come with a crown).

But wait, there's more! Not only does Justin serve up some fantastic brews, but he also has top-notch treats at the ready when you walk in. I mean, who doesn't love a place that caters to both humans and their four-legged companions? The vibe is laid back and chill, and it's not unusual for the pawrents to make friends while I'm busy charming everyone for snacks.

And if you're unsure what to try next, Justin is your go-to guy for beer recommendations. Next week, we'll be diving into Frog Rock, his first recommendation to us! Thanks for the intro Justin, because it hopped right into my favorites list!

So, if you're looking for a brewery where the beer flows like a river and the humans are as friendly as a pack of puppies, Skint Chestnut is the place to be! Cheers to good brews, great company, and the best treats around! 🍻🐾
