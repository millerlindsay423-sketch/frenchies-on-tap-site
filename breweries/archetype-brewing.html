Archetype Brewing

🐾🐾🐾🐾 (3.5 / 5)

"This spot? Pretty solid!"

🐾🍻 Alright, fur-friends, listen up—I've got another brewery adventure to report, and this one had me sniffing out snacks and sours like a pro!

This spot? Pretty solid! The taproom was wide open (perfect for a little casual zooming), and let me tell you—those super comfy sofas were calling my name! A prime spot for lounging or investigating, because where there are sofas, there are snacks hiding underneath. And where there are kids, well… there's at least a 75% chance someone's dropping something delicious. 🕵️‍♂️🐾

Now, my humans were winding down for the day, so they weren't going full send on the strong stuff. Their pick? False Antagonist, a Sour – Traditional Gose. And let me tell you, my human almost gave this one a 4-paw rating! It had all the juicy goodness without too much pucker—just the right level of tart and tasty.

Would I come back? Oh, absolutely! Cozy vibes, plenty of sniff-worthy corners, and prime snack-hunting opportunities? Sign me up! 🐾🍻
